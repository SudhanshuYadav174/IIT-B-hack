About Votra

Votra is revolutionizing the way remote teams collaborate and stay productive. Our platform provides all the tools you need to manage distributed teams effectively, no matter where your team members are located.

Founded in 2020, we've helped thousands of companies transition to remote work seamlessly. Our mission is to make remote work not just possible, but better than traditional office work.

With features designed specifically for distributed teams, Votra helps you maintain productivity, foster collaboration, and keep your team engaged no matter where they're working from.
